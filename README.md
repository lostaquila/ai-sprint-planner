Momentum AI – AI-Powered Sprint Planning for Product Teams 🚀

Momentum AI is an intelligent sprint planning assistant designed to simplify and accelerate the product development workflow. It transforms raw product specs into actionable tickets, helps you plan sprints based on team capacity, and keeps everything organized in a beautiful Kanban-style interface.

✨ Features

📝 Generate tickets from product specs
Automatically extract structured Agile tickets from freeform PRDs or descriptions using AI.

📊 Sprint planning powered by AI
Plan sprints based on capacity and priorities with just one click — let AI do the heavy lifting.

📋 Kanban-style board
Visualize and manage tickets across statuses like Backlog, In Sprint, In Progress, and Done.

📂 Documentation section
Built-in project documentation to guide contributors and stakeholders.

🧠 AI Workflows

Two agentic AI workflows built with n8n
:

Generate Tickets – Translates product specs into structured ticket objects.

Sprint Planner – Picks the best-fit tickets from backlog based on available sprint points.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend: Next.js, TailwindCSS

Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth)

AI Integration: OpenRouter (LLMs) via n8n

Deployment: Vercel
